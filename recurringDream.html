Grunto's Crazy Breakfast

Grunto Wakes up on a cold morning and has a large hankering for hard boiled eggs. After many hours of searching, Grunto realizes that has no hard boiled eggs. Grunto is sad. Grunto turns on the Juice world and sits on his bed to cry for several days. Legend says that the rivers run because Grunto never got his hard boiled eggs.
